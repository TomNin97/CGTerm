This is a demonstration of multiple shapes!

Here we have a a scene devised of multiple shapes mostly similar to those shown in the previous pages.

The castle itself is made of multiple cubes with 2d objects representing the window and doors.

The towers are comprised of cylinders with cones for roofs, the mountains consist of two cones each, and the trees consist of cylinders and roughly spherical shapes

To render multiple objects at the same time in a canvas, multiple buffers are required. Each object uses a declared buffer, a color uniform, and matrixes for translating the coordinates

Then, the buffers, uniforms, and matrices are utilized to draw the object. Finally, the program is called to draw the scene

Sliders below the canvas can be used to move the camera around the scene
